## Purchasing Your Server

Follow these simple steps to purchase and configure your game server:

### Step 1: Access the Billing Dashboard

Log into your billing account to access the dashboard. You'll see all navigation options that allow you to manage various tasks and browse available server options.

### Step 2: Navigate to Server Products

Once in the dashboard, locate the section dedicated to server purchases. This area displays all available server options, configurations, and pricing plans.

Look for the **"Order New Services"** or **"Game Servers"** section in the main navigation menu.

**[Image Placeholder: Server Products Navigation]**

### Step 3: Choose Your Server Configuration

Now it's time to select your server specifications. This step is crucial for optimal performance.

#### A. Select Your Server Location

Choose the data center location closest to your primary player base for the best performance.

<Tip>
  **Pro Tip:** Select your server location based on the region with the **lowest ping** from your country or your players' locations. This ensures the best possible speed and connection quality.
</Tip>

**Available Locations:**

- Asia-Pacific (Singapore, Tokyo, Mumbai)
- North America (New York, Los Angeles)
- Europe (London, Frankfurt)

**[Image Placeholder: Location Selection Interface]**

---

#### B. Select Your Server Specifications

Choose the specifications that best match your needs and budget. Consider the following:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="CPU" icon="microchip">
    Ensure enough processing power for your game or application. Higher CPU performance = smoother gameplay.
  </Card>
  <Card title="RAM" icon="memory">
    Choose sufficient memory to handle player loads and performance demands. More RAM = more players.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Storage" icon="hard-drive">
    Select SSD for faster load times or HDD for larger capacity at lower cost.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Bandwidth" icon="network-wired">
    Ensure enough data transfer to support your expected traffic and player base.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

**Additional Considerations:**

- **Backups**: Automatic backups to protect your data
- **DDoS Protection**: Keep your server safe from attacks
- **Control Panel**: Easier management and configuration
- **Mod Support**: Ensure compatibility with your desired plugins and mods

<Note>
  Not sure what specifications you need? Our team can help you choose the right plan based on your expected player count and game type. Contact support for personalized recommendations.
</Note>

## Accessing Your New Server

After successfully purchasing your server, you can access and manage it by following these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Dashboard">
    Log into your billing panel and navigate to the main dashboard
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Your Active Services">
    Look for the "Services" or "My Services" section in the navigation menu
  </Step>
  <Step title="View All Services">
    Click on "View All" to see your complete list of active servers
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Your Server">
    Find and click on your newly purchased service from the list
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Server Management">
    Click **"Go to Server"** to start managing and configuring your server
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Happens After Purchase?

Once your payment is confirmed, here's what to expect:

**Immediate (0-5 minutes):**

- Order confirmation email sent to your registered email
- Payment receipt generated
- Server provisioning begins automatically

**Within 15 minutes:**

- Server fully provisioned and online
- Game panel account created automatically
- Welcome email with server details and credentials sent
- Server appears in your services dashboard

**Your Next Steps:**

1. Check your email for game panel credentials
2. Log into the game panel using the provided link
3. Configure your server settings
4. Install mods or plugins (optional)
5. Share connection details with your players
6. Start playing!

  **First time setting up a game server?** Check out our detailed game-specific setup guides in the documentation to get started quickly.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How long does server provisioning take?

Most servers are provisioned automatically within 5-15 minutes after payment confirmation. During peak times, it may take up to 30 minutes. You'll receive an email once your server is ready.

---

### Can I upgrade my server later?

Yes! You can upgrade your server specifications at any time through the billing panel. Navigate to your service and select the upgrade option. Resources can be scaled up (or down) based on your needs.

---

### What if I need to change my server location?

Server location changes require migration. Contact our support team, and we'll assist you with migrating your server to a different data center.

---

### Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a refund policy within the first 72 hours if you're not satisfied with our service. Please review our full refund policy in the billing panel or contact support for details.

---

### How do I cancel my server?

You can cancel your server anytime through the billing panel. Navigate to your service, click on "Request Cancellation," and follow the prompts. Your server will remain active until the end of your current billing period.
